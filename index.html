About Us

HelpCare Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization founded on the principle that helping others is good for society.We take a unique approach to help people based on the principle that helping others is good for both the donor and recipient. Rather than pooling donations from large numbers of people, we leverage technology to facilitate one-to-one connections. Donors are connected directly with donees and charity organizations and can track their donation from end-to-end.
